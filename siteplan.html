Site Plan for Gran Sabana Adventures

This document outlines the plan for the Gran Sabana Adventures website, a hub for hiking enthusiasts in the Gran Sabana region.

Site Name

Gran Sabana Adventures

This name evokes exploration, nature, and adventure in a serene valley setting, making it memorable and relevant for outdoor enthusiasts. It positions the site as a go-to resource for hiking and related activities.

Optional domain availability: gransabana-adventures.com

[Logo: Stylized mountain icon with "Green Valley Adventures" text]

Site Purpose

The site serves as a comprehensive hub for the Gran Sabana hiking community, providing detailed information on local trails, hiking tips, etc. It aims to promote safe outdoor activities, connect hikers, and encourage environmental stewardship in the Gran Sabana area.

Scenarios

These scenarios represent questions from the target audience (outdoor enthusiasts, beginners to experienced hikers) to guide content development:

  • What are the best beginner-friendly hiking trails in Gran Sabana? – This will be addressed with a dedicated trails page featuring maps, difficulty ratings, and safety tips.
  • How can I join the Gran Sabana Adventures club and participate in group hikes? – This will link to a membership page with a sign-up form, event listings, and contact information.

Color Schema

The color schema is designed to evoke nature and tranquility, aligning with the outdoor theme:

  • Forest Green (#228B22): Used for headings, navigation links, and accent elements to represent the lush greenery of the valley.
  • Light Gray (#D3D3D3): Used for background colors on sections and footers to provide a clean, readable contrast.

This schema is applied throughout this document for demonstration.

Typography

The typography focuses on readability and a modern, outdoor feel:

  • Roboto (sans-serif): Used for headings (h1-h6) to provide a bold, clean look that stands out.
  • Arial (sans-serif): Used for body text and paragraphs to ensure high readability across devices.

This typography is applied throughout this document for demonstration.
